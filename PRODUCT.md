# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

- **Citizens**: Individuals seeking to report civic or rural grievances (e.g., municipal issues, agricultural problems, disaster assistance), track their status, and receive alerts or information on government schemes.
- **Officials / Administrators**: Government authorities who view, track, analyze, and resolve incoming grievances and inspect analytics.

## Product Purpose

To provide a trustworthy, simple, and community-focused portal that enables public grievance submission, real-time tracking, and efficient resolution.

## Positioning

A centralized, accessible, AI-assisted platform making civic grievance reporting transparent and approachable for every citizen.

## Brand Personality

- Trustworthy and professional
- Accessible and friendly
- Simple and community-focused

## Anti-references

- Overly complex enterprise software layouts with cluttered sidebars and dense menus.
- Bland, dark-mode SaaS templates that look like financial dashboards.
- Slow, confusing, or unapproachable legacy government interfaces.

## Design Principles

- **Clarity Over Density**: Present only the most important actions and information on screen, reducing cognitive load.
- **Inclusive Accessibility**: Design with high color contrast, readable text layouts, and clean visual hierarchies suitable for screen-reader and voice workflows.
- **Empathetic Feedback**: Provide clear status indicators and positive reinforcement so users feel their concerns are being addressed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Adherence to standard web accessibility guidelines (aiming for WCAG 2.1 AA).
- Clear, highly readable text with strong contrast ratios.
